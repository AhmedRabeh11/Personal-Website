ABO​UT SCHOOL

Alexandria STEM high school 

This place is special...

In comparison to many traditional schools in Egypt, the key distinction that sets Alexandria STEM School apart from the other schools is the focus on the extraction of well-rounded students who will be able to make the best possible contribution to society. STEM Schools are seeking to incorporate leadership, academic achievement, strong research skills, fine arts, and sports excellence in one student. In an attempt to achieve the previous result, STEM schools have developed a curriculum that is based on four main aspects: science, humanities, art, and sports. Moreover, after discussing each scientific concept, students are asked to carry out experiments in the school's labs to understand the concepts well and improve their experimental skills for future research careers. 

As far as humanities are concerned, students take classes in languages, social studies, civics, and religion. And in order to foster their sensational and artistic tastes, students are required to take part in one of three activities: Art, Library, and Music. Besides, Sport is another significant focal point for our school because of their total agreement that sports play an obvious role in building up student bodies and minds. Finally, STEM requires its students to build a capstone project every semester in order to build their leadership and research skills. Personally, I am proud to be a part of this fascinating culture that values science and strives to produce talented, well-trained young scientists who can influence whatever community they exist in.
