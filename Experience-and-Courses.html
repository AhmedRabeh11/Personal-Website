Our game consists of an App/MOBA game where schools can have a platform to upload the teachers coursework and lectures and students would use the same platform to attend sessions and watch lectures, the progress of students on the sessions/coursework part would then transfer to the MOBA game side of the app where students can be awarded for their effort by leveling up, building kingdoms and attacking other class mates from the same school in the game the game will functions similarly to other famous MOBA games like (LOL) and clash of clans only difference is that progress in the game is directly linked to the students’ progress and achievements in his/her coursework , this allows students to have a motivational aspect in their education where they have something to strive and encourage them to attend online sessions in order to rank higher on the leaderboard with respect to other students, not only will the solution operate as a motivational tool to the students in terms of scoring and increasing the competitiveness between students the solution will also offer a platform for schools and educational institutes to monitor each student individually his/her progress, attendance minutes and overall scoring on his/her class or school. Leader/ researcher
Capstone project
9/2018 - present

The capstone project, which we are required to do every semester in our three years at Alexandria STEM school, is a multifaceted assignment that serves as a culminating academic and intellectual experience for students. Every semester, the Egyptian ministry of education provides us with information about one of the challenges that face Egypt, and we have to find a creative solution for it. Duration of one capstone project: 3 months.

The capstone project consists of four main parts:·   

Portfolio : A scientific research paper where we write all the data we have collected, hypotheses, results, analyses, solutions, and a conclusion. · 

     Poster: A form of printed visual communication, presenting a summary of the whole capstone project. It is usually a 70cm by 100cm, landscape orientation with fourcolumns that allow proper space for segmentation. ·

      Prototype: An initial creation of the product that has the ability to solve the capstone challenge. Each prototype must pass the test requirements, which the Egyptian ministry of education approved, to consider it an applicable product in the real world.
  
     Exhibition day: A day at the end of each semester in which students present their whole work in front of 4-5 professors from high universities in Egypt.

    My capstone projects:     
      
 Capstone Challenge: improving the usage of arid areas/ Recycling garbage and retaining garbage for recycling. (Our capstone written poster)   ·     Capstone Challenge: improving the use of alternative energy and solving the energy crisis. ( OURCAPSTONE WRITTEN POSTER) ·   

   Capstone Challenge: Removing Microplastics from water to reduce water pollution and improve the aquatic life. (Our capstone written poster)
 
     Capstone Challenge: research paper about solving the Water Scarcity problem. (Click here to see our research paper)

Introduction to Quantum Computing Course
with IBM Quantum.

2020-2021 

This is an introductory course in quantum computing. The aim is to expose students to this emerging field while providing them with real-world programming skills for quantum computers. In the course, students will learn about the foundational concepts in quantum computation, including superposition, entanglement, superdense coding, and quantum teleportation. The first half of the course will focus on the necessary prerequisites that students need to delve into quantum computation, such as math, programming, and physics concepts. The second half of the course will introduce students to the foundational concepts in quantum computing.
